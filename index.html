Before Startup Weekend


Signup for Free!

More Info

The New Haven Startup Tour provides the opportunity to look behind the scenes of New Haven area startups. You will get a chance to visit each company (usually in their offices) and meet the founders, talk to employees, and discover a whole world of opportunity most never knew existed in the New Haven area. Best of all it's FREE!

Please choose one company per time slot to visit and be mindful of travel time to physically move between the locations. You may mix and match from the different tracks if you prefer.
